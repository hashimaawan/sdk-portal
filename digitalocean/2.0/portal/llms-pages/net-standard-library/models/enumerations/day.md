# Day

Source: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hashimaawan/sdk-portal/main/digitalocean/2.0/portal/#/net-standard-library/x-redirect/JTI0bSUyRkRheQ

The day of the maintenance window policy. May be one of `monday` through `sunday`, or `any` to indicate an arbitrary week day.
